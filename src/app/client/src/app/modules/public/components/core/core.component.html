Explore Core Values

Restoring Agency:

Create an enabling environment for individuals and institutions to exercise choice and imagination.

Resolving for Scale:

Engage with problems from multiple perspectives and improve the adoption of relevant solutions by all segments of society.

Catalysing Networks:

Facilitate interactions between state, civil society and markets to leverage existing infrastructure (services and facilities).

Distributing ability to solve:

Enhance the capabilities of diverse entities to respond quickly and directly with contextual solutions.

Inspiring Co-creation:

Create an enabling environment for all actors to interact and collaborate in innovating relevant solutions.

Developing Public Goods:

Make scarce resources (e.g., knowledge, expertise, data) abundant in an open and equitable manner.

Distributing value creation:

Enable all actors of the society to create, share, as well as make use of the value produced.

Embracing rapid evolution:

Retain flexibility to develop new capabilities for all entities by rapidly generalising specific solutions and innovations.

Demonstrating system leadership:

Nurture a leadership ecosystem that anticipates and co-creates solutions based on a systemic view of the challenges.

Empowering with data:

Help stakeholders use data to discover new problems to solve, or to make existing solutions more effective.
